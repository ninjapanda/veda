Charity Donations

The subject is a non-profit organization with deep ties to the philanthropic community in East and Southeast Asia. The challenge is to build a platform that protects the identity of donors while providing the means for taxation and auditing, and to keep charity spending accountable and transparent.

Expenses & Claims

The subject is a production firm in the film and entertainment industry in East Asia. The challenge is to build a system for reporting and auditing expenses by contractors to reduce the risk of fraud and increase the submission and settlement throughput of expense claims by third parties.

Intellectual Property

The subject is an intellectual property practice chiefly operating in North America, East Asia, and Southeast Asia. The challenge is to build a platform for anonymous and accountable asset management with seamless permission-based access controls for clients or their legal representatives.

Loyalty Programme

The subject is a listed company in the fast-moving consumer goods industry with market operations in Greater China. The challenge is to build comprehensive profiles of consumers and consumption patterns within the current and potential legal and regulatory limits of the relevant jurisdictions, such as GDPR.

Medical Insurance Claims

The subject is a listed company in the insurance industry with market operations know Greater China.
The challenge is to build a platform for secure and frictionless processing of medical insurance claims while preventing multiple-claims fraud and mitigating other types of insurance claims fraud.

Recycling Programme

The subject is a non-profit organization dedicated to environmental and sustainability causes around the world. The challenge is to build a system that tracks fast-moving consumer goods and provides incentive for participants across the products' life cycle to dispose of the waste in a sustainable and environmentally-friendly fashion.

Supply Chain & Logistics

The subject is a listed company in the fast-moving consumer goods industry with market operations in Greater China. The challenge is to build a trusted network as a form of human-machine symbiosis of quality control checkpoints to bring unprecedented accountability to food safety while boosting fulfilment efficiency.

Virtual Banking Services

The subject has relevant banking and financial operator licences in various jurisdictions in West Asia, East Asia, and Southeast Asia. The challenge is to build a banking institution without any physical branches in any of its target markets with legal and compliant operations across relevant jurisdictions, especially in KYC, AML, and CFT.
