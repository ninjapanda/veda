What is VEDA?

VEDA is a privacy-preserving identity information stewardship system
designed for legal and compliant enterprise applications.

Why VEDA?

Chain-Agnostic

VEDA brings the best compliance, administrative, and transactional tools to any blockchain or distributed ledger without the burden of resource-intensive integrations or migrations.

Future-proof Protection

Security is our highest priority. Cryptography is our religion. With some of the world's top researchers, we baked in core tenets of privacy, accountability, and protection into our proprietary library.

Legal and Compliant

Blockchains cannot operate outside the scope of legal and regulatory frameworks. Though disciplined technical and legal design, VEDA provides legitimacy to all blockchains and distributed ledgers.

Trusted and Useful

VEDA is partnered with venture-backed private companies and listed public companies worldwide building decentralised applications or dapp platforms across a wide variety of industry verticals.
